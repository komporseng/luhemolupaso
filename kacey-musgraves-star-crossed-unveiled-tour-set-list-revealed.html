Kacey Musgraves Star-Crossed: Unveiled Tour Set List Revealed

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, May 9, 2024

Kacey Musgraves is hitting the road!

The 33-year-old star-crossed singer-songwriter launched her Star-Crossed: Unveiled Tour on Wednesday night (January 19) in St. Paul, Minn.

Click inside to read more…

The 15-date trek, which wraps in Toronto at the end of February, is in support of her latest studio album, Star-Crossed, and features a ton of songs from the record – as well a a cover of TLC during her “Kaceyoke” section!

At the end of last year, Kacey Musgraves got candid about the decision behind her divorce. Find out what she had to say.

Check out the set list for the Star-Crossed: Unveiled Tour…

1. Star-Crossed
2. Good Wife
3. Cherry Blossom
4. Simple Times
5. Breadwinner
6. Golden Hour
7. Butterflies
8. Lonely Weekend
9. Space Cowboy
10. High Horse
11. Camera Roll
12. Hookup Scene
13. Merry Go ‘Round
14. Kaceyoke (Opening night: “No Scrubs,” TLC)
15. Justified
16. There Is a Light
17. Gracias a la Vida
ENCORE:
18. Slow Burn
19. Rainbow
