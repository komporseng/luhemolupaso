Who Is Blue Kim? Her Family, Dating, Height

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, July 21, 2024

Blue Kim immediately standouts among the Big Brother season 25 houseguests. Long before the show even premiered in August 2023, many began declaring Kim as their pick to go far.

Keep on reading to learn about Kim’s parents, boyfriend, career, and more!

Blue Kim On Big Brother 25

With $750K on the line, Blue Kim joined fellow houseguests in the Big Brother villa. Like the rest, she seemed confident about her chances.

Opening up about her decision to appear on the show, Kim shared, “I want to be on Big Brother because Big Brother requires two things: Strategy and a social game, which honestly, I have both. And the money won’t hurt!”

She also shared her strategy going into the game by revealing, “My strategy going into the game is lying about my job so they don’t know I’m a strategist, winning the competitions, and playing a mean social game.”

Days before Big Brother Season 25 premiered, Kim took to IG and announced, “I’VE BEEN KEEPING THIS A SECRET SINCE MARCH BUT I AM EXCITED TO FINALLY ANNOUNCE THAT I AM A CONTESTANT ON THE 25TH SEASON OF BIG BROTHER.”

In the same post, Kim thanked the Big Brother team, friends, and family for their support.

Blue Kim Job

Big Brother starlet Blue Kim is a brand strategist. More specifically, she is an Associate Manager of Strategy and Communication at NVE Experience Agency in New York. She first joined the agency in August 2021 as a Strategy and Communication Coordinator.

Between Jan 2021 and Sep 2021, Kim served as a Public Relations Coordinator at the Collegiate Association for Artists of Color. Around the same time, she was working as a Junior Strategist at Giant Spoon and Junior DEI Consultant at Cineclusion Consulting.

Kim began her career interning at the Corona Chamber of Commerce, Siempre Golden, WeWork, Fabletics, and Sony Pictures Entertainment. She also had short stints at Rebecca Taylor as a Luxury Sales Keyholder. Sam Edelman as an Assistant Sales Manager, and ALEX AND ANI as a Sales Specialist.

When quizzed about her pursuit of marketing and events, Kim answered, “I started to wonder what people would categorize as important parts of their lives and how I can create that for someone.”

According to her LinkedIn, Kim is Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion Advocate.

Blue Kim Education

Blue Kim holds a Bachelor of Arts in Strategic and Corporate Communications from Chapman University. While at Chapman, she served as the Director of Community Service in the Alpha Phi fraternity. She was also a member of the Black Student Union.

In her graduation post, Kim had a message for her Alma Mater that read, “Thank you, Chapman, for all the memories made and the wonderful people I got to cross paths with. Though I still challenge Chapman to do better, the people I have met through Chapman have made me better and I couldn’t thank you enough.”

Did You Know: Kim served as a Summer Camp Counselor for Camp4Kidz in 2017.

How Old Is Blue Kim?

Blue Kim was born in August 1998, making her 25 years old when Big Brother 25 aired.

Blue Kim Family

Big Brother’s Blue Kim is a first-generation Korean-American. She is proud of her heritage. However, that wasn’t always true.

In a 2020 Instagram post, Kim opened up about her struggles. She wrote, “I remember yelling at my mom to pack me a turkey sandwich (I don’t even like turkey sandwiches lol) for lunch because the kids at school would tell me how my Korean lunches smelled. Like many others who attended predominantly white schools for most of their lives, I tried to hide my cultural identity and background to assimilate to an American school.”

Kim is the oldest daughter of her family. Her baby sister turned 21 in December 2021.

Blue Kim Height

Brand strategist-turned-Reality TV star Blue Kim should be no more than 5’3” in height.

Kim has a unique look, accentuated by her multiple meaningful tattoos. She also relies on cleverly-done makeup to express her style.

Back in 2021, Kim underwent Laisk eye surgery in South Korea. She even documented the entire procedure in her TikTok video.

Is Blue Kim Dating Anyone?

Regarding her dating life and relationship status, Blue Kim’s social media posts don’t give away much. It is difficult to conclude if she has a boyfriend or potential romantic partner.
