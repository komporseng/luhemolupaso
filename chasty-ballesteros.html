Untold Facts About Hairstyle Turned Actress

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, July 29, 2024

If you have watched the 2021 horror thriller Malibu Horror Story you might recognize Chasty Ballesteros. She is a hairstylist turned actress well-known among the public for her role as Tanya Showers in the TV series The Ranch. 

She began her acting career with a small role portraying the nurse character in the action-adventure and drama TV series Smallville. As of now, she has become one of the famous faces in Hollywood. 

Chasty Is Married To a Mountain Biker

Me, Myself and I actress Chasty Ballesteros is currently married to her long-term boyfriend, Curtis Keene. Her husband is a professional mountain biker. She often shares her photos along with her husband on her Instagram handle.

Moving on to their dating history, the couple fell in love with each other on their first met and soon began dating. After some years of living in a relationship, they got engaged to each other and tied their knot after one and a half years of their engagement ceremony.

On November 13, 2017, the romantic couple tied their knot in a huge wedding ceremony in the presence of their family member, friends, and some close ones.

Net Worth: Chasty Enjoys $2 Million 

Chasty Ballesteros has a total net worth of $2 million, like Leeanna Walsman. She has made this huge fortune through her successful career as an actress, hairstylist, and model.

On the other hand, Chasty earns a decent from her profile on various social media handles, including Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter, where she has a good fan following. 

Moreover, she has endorsed and appeared in ads for several famous brands, including her advertisement for the famous motorcycle company Honda.

Career Overview

Chasty Ballesteros started off her professional career as a hairstylist immediately after completing her high school graduation from a private school in her hometown. During her prime days as a hairstylist, she also worked as a manager in a famous hair salon.

After some years, she moved her interest towards childhood interest, modelling, and appeared in many commercials, including Kokanee, Credit Unions of BC, etc. She soon became a well-known model in the town. 

After some time, she got involved in the acting profession. Finally, in 2009, she began her on-screen acting career with a small role portraying the nurse character in the action-adventure and drama TV series Smallville. 

Following it, she has portrayed several roles in various movies and TV series of different genres. According to her IMDb profile, she has all together appeared in more than a hundred different movies and TV series.
