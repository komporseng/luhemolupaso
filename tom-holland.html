Tom Holland - Bio, Age, Single, Nationality, Net Worth, Facts

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, May 22, 2024

Table of Biography

Tom Holland is a dancer and actor from England. Tom Holland is best known for his role as Spider-Man in the Marvel Cinematic Universe. He has appeared in Captain America: Civil War, Spider-Man: Homecoming, Avengers: Infinity War, Avengers: Endgame, and Spider-Man: Far From Home as Peter Parker / Spider-Man.

Early Life

Caption: Tom Holland’s childhood photo with his brothers (Source: Instagram)

Tom Holland was born on June 1, 1996, in Kingston upon Thames, England, UK, and is 25 years old. Thomas Stanley Holland is his full name. He is the eldest son of photographer Nicola Elizabeth and comedian and author Dominic Holland. Tom has three brothers: twins Sam and Harry Holland, as well as Paddy Holland. He is British and English, and he is of English, Irish, and Manx ancestry. Holland started dancing when he was a child. He started performing as a child in a hip-hop class at Nifty Feet Dance School in Wimbledon. Lynne Page, a choreographer, first saw him when he was a child.

Tom finished his schooling at Donhead Preparatory School in Wimbledon, South West London. Following that, he enrolled at Wimbledon College, a voluntary aided Jesuit Roman Catholic comprehensive school. Following that, he attended The BRIT School of Performing Arts and Technology.

Professional life

Caption: Tom Holland as a Spiderman in Avengers Infinity War (Source: Instagram)

In 2008, Tom Holland started his acting career as Billy Elliot / Michael in the stage musical Billy Elliot the Musical at Victoria Palace Theatre. He played the role in the musical until 2010. In the 2012 film The Impossible, he played Lucas Bennett for the first time. In 2012, he received the Hollywood Film Awards and the St. Louis Film Critics Association award for his performance in the film. Likewise, In addition, for the same film, he received Young Artist Awards and Saturn Awards for Best Performance by a Younger Actor in 2013. He has also starred in several films, including How I Live Now in 2013, In the Heart of the Sea in 2015, and A Monster Calls in 2016.

Spiderman

In 2016, Tom appeared as Peter Parker / Spider-Man in the Marvel Cinematic Universe. He first appeared as Spider-Man in Captain America: Civil War in 2016. Despite his small screen time, he received positive feedback for his portrayal as the famous web-slinger. It paved the way for his highly awaited and well-received appearance in Spider-Man: Homecoming in 2017. In 2017, he reprised his role as Spider-Man in Spider-Man: Homecoming, reprising his role in Captain America: Civil War.

In 2016, he was named Breakthrough Performance of the Year at the Golden Schmoes Awards for Captain America: Civil War. For the same film, he also received a Saturn Award for Best Performance by a Younger Actor. Holland then reprised his superhero character in the 2018 and 2019 superhero blockbusters Avengers: Infinity War and Avengers: Endgame. And, in 2019, he added the next chapter to his character’s story with Spider-Man: Far From Home.

Meanwhile, Holland has other acting commitments. In 2015, the epic In the Heart of the Sea was a box-office flop. However, it offered another platform for the rising star, as well as the opportunity to put his skills to the test in difficult shooting environments. He then appeared in the 2016 films Edge of Winter and The Lost City of Z eventually appearing in the 2017 medieval action drama Pilgrimage. Holland collaborated with Will Smith on the animated adventure Spies in Disguise, which was released in late 2019. Holland began production on Spider-Man: No Way Home in October 2020, and the shooting was completed at the end of March 2021.

Achievements and Net Worth

For his first acting appearance in a film, Tom Holland won several awards and nominations. He has earned nine coveted awards for his outstanding role as 12-year-old Lucas Bennett in The Impossible. He earned the Spotlight Award, Best Youth Performance, Breakthrough Performance – Male, Young British Performer of the Year, Best Male Newcomer, and Best Performance in a Feature Film for his work in The Impossible.

Tom received the Breakthrough Performance of the Year award at the Golden Schmoes Awards in 2016 for his role in Captain America: Civil War. In 2017, he received the prestigious British Academy Film Awards’ Rising Star Award (BAFTA). At the 2017 Saturn Awards, he won Best Performance by a Younger Actor for Captain America: Civil War. He won Choice Summer Movie Actor at the famous Teen Choice Awards the same year for his appearance in Spider-Man: Homecoming.

Tom received Teen Choice Awards and Saturn Awards for his role in Spider-Man: Far From Home. Moreover, Tom has also received the Kids’ Choice Awards for Avengers: Endgame. Tom Holland has a $15 million net worth, but his pay is unspecified.
