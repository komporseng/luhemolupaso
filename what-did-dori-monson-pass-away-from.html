What did Dori Monson pass away from?

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, July 15, 2024

The Dori Monson Show, an afternoon discussion radio program in Seattle, Washington, on KIRO-FM, was hosted by Dori Monson, an American radio personality. Monson, a Seattle resident of Icelandic ancestry, was brought up in the predominantly Scandinavian neighborhood of Ballard.

He completed high school at Ballard. He worked as the play-by-play announcer for Husky football on campus broadcaster KCMU while a student at the University of Washington.
