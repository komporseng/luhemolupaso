    Herman Tømmeraas Facts

  • He has served his mandatory military service.
  • In 2015, Herman Tømmeraas was cast in the recurring role of Christoffer “Penetrator-Chris” Schistad, the on-and-off love interest of Eva, in the teen drama web series, Skam, which starred Lisa Teige, Josefine Frida Pettersen, Tarjei Sandvik Moe, and Iman Meskini.
  • As a producer, he has worked on a couple of episodes of the drama TV series, Semester.
  • Herman Tømmeraas has also provided his vocals to the character named Walter Beckett, a socially inept scientific genius who graduated from MIT at the age of 15 and designs gadgets, in the Norwegian-dubbed version of the 2019 American computer-animated spy comedy film, Spies in Disguise. It is Tom Holland who has done voiceover work for the same character in the English version.
  • In 2020, he co-starred as Fjor, the high-school-aged “son” in the Jutul family of Jötunn from Norse mythology, in the Netflix drama series, Ragnarok. The other lead cast members on the show include actors like David Stakston, Jonas Strand Gravli, Theresa Frostad Eggesbø, Henriette Steenstrup, Gísli Örn Garðarsson, and Synnøve Macody Lund.
