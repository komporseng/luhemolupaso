Kelly Carlson

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, May 11, 2024

Kelly Carlson is an American actress and model who is best known for her role as Kimber Henry in the FX drama series, “Nip/Tuck”. Kelly actually got her breakthrough with the series that earned several nominations and awards as well. Born in Minneapolis, and raised in Bloomington, Minnesota, Kelly began her career as an actress in a minor role in “3000 Miles to Graceland”(2001).

Carlson was named to Tear Sheet Magazine’s 50 Most Beautiful list in 2001. Carlson is also active on social media, having an Instagram account called @therealkellycarlson.

Where was Kelly Carlson Born?

Kelly Carlson was born in Minneapolis, Minnesota, in the United States on February 17, 1976. Kelly Lee Carlson is her given name. Her country of origin is the United States of America. Carlson is of mixed ethnicity (Swedish, German, Irish, English, and Norwegian), and her zodiac sign is Aquarius.

Kelly was born in Sweden to Swedish parents Lee Dixon Carlson (father) and Mary Joan Carlson (mother) (mother). Her mother was a hairdresser and her father was a high school basketball coach. Because her father works in the sports industry, she has had an interest in sports since she was a child. She was an equestrian rider as early as the age of seven. She even developed an interest in the performing arts and eventually decided to pursue a career as an actor.

Kelly’s family relocated to Bloomington when she was a teenager, then to Richfield, where she graduated from Academy of Holy Angels and began working in theatre. Kelly began modeling at the age of 17 and has since starred in advertisements for Miller Lite, Rembrandt, and Oliver Peoples Sunglasses, owing to her toned physical figure.

 Career Highlights:

  • Kelly Carlson began her earlier career in show business as a model as she even appeared in Maxim Magazine(August 2004) and on the cover of Stuff Magazine(October 2004).
  • Carlson then started to appear in the theatre where she performed in productions of Vanities, Cheaters, Girls Guide to Chaos, Charlotte’s Web, and Can’t Trust the Mate.
  • Carlson made her acting debut in 2001, with a minor role as a motorcycle gang member in the action-adventure crime film titled “3000 Miles to Graceland”.
  • She was then landed with the role of Kimberly Henry in the FX drama series, “Nip/Tuck” in 2003, that eventually turned out to be her breakthrough role as she later became the series regular and remained in the show until its last season till 2010.
  • In 2004, she played a small role in Ada in the drama series titled “Everwood”.
  • She has appeared in several feature films, including “Paparazzi”(2004), “Starship Troopers 2: Hero of the Federation”(2004), and “Made of Honor”(2008).
  • Kelly Carlson has also made guest spots on television series including “CSI: Crime Scene Investigation”(2006), “CSI: Miami”(2007), “Monk”(2009), and “The Finder”(2012).
  • She also had a short appearance in the 2009 Canadian television film, “Degrassi Goes Hollywood”.
  • In 2006, she had a huge film break playing one of the leading roles as Kate Triton in the action film titled “The Marine” alongside John Cena.
  • She was also seen in “Ghostfacers”, an online spin-off of The CW dark fantasy series Supernatural in 2010.
  • Besides, Carlson is the spokesperson for a Minnesota-based humanitarian organization, Smile Network International.
