An American television personality Steelo Brim is also an actor and host. He is mostly known for co-hosting one of the famous MTV TV show called Ridiculousness since 2011.

Early Life and Education

Steelo was born on June 5, 1998, in Chicago, Illinois to parents Frank Brim, who is a Chicago Fire Department Battalion Chief and his mother Tracy Brim, who is a pastor. He belongs to an Afro-American ethnic background and has American nationality.

The handsome host is a graduate of Whitney M. Young Magnet High School in West Side, Chicago, Illinois, United States. For his further studies, he joined Morgan State University in Baltimore. Later, Brim moved to LA at the age of 19 to pursue his career in the music industry. Although it didn’t work out for him he got other opportunities through it.
