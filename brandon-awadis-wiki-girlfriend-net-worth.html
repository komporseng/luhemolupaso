How Old Is Brandon Awadis?

As of September 2020, Brandon Awadis is 25 years old. He was born in 1995 in San Diego, California, in the United States, and his birthday is on 23 June.

Brandon is a son of Ron Awadis and Sana Awadis and grew up with his brother named Brian Awadis. All his family members are social media stars, and they pursue their careers as YouTubers.

Concerning his physique, he stands at the height of 5 feet and 10 inches and weighs 70 kg or 154.5 lbs in weight.

Who Is Brandon Awadis Dating?

The 25 years old YouTuber Brandon Awadis is currently single and does not depict any signs of romantic spheres. But he has been sharing the videos entitled 'Meet My Girlfriend' in September 2020, where he featured Vanessa Lyon. Following the video, several rumors have flaunted regarding the possible romances between the alleged lovebirds. However, neither of the couples has confirmed the story.

However, he was in a romantic relationship with his ex-girlfriend named Jackie Figueroa, an NBA enthusiast, and social media influencer. The former lovebirds had been dating since 2015. However, their relationship had been on/off. And during their two-year relationship, they collaborated on several social media contents of their own.

Unlike Awadis, Figueroa appears to have moved on from him. On 4 September 2020, Awadis posted a video entitled 'She is leaving me for another guy,' where he featured his ex-girlfriend.
