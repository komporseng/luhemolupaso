Kelly Clarkson's Husband, Brandon Blackstock Was Previously Married Before Melissa Ashworth; Shares

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, July 16, 2024

Brandon Blackstock is an American talent manager, probably known for his work like Shelton Live: It's All About Tonight (2010) and The Kelly Clarkson Show (2019). In addition, he is also famous as the life partner of the winner of American Idol 2002, Kelly Clarkson. 

Star's spouse Blackstock was born on December 16, 1976, as Brandon Narvel Blackstock in Fort Worth, Texas, the United States. He holds American citizenship and is of white ethnicity. Moreover, he is the son of Elisa Gayle Ritter and Narvel Blackstock.

Brandon and Kelly's Wedding

On October 20, 2013, Blackstock and Clarkson wedded at Blackberry Farm in Walland, Tennessee. The beautiful bride even shared this news on Twitter, saying, 

I'm officially Mrs. Blackstock :) We got married yesterday at Blackberry Farms in TN, the most beautiful place ever.

During the big day, the only guests in attendance were Brandon’s two kids from a previous marriage. The television host of The Kelly Clarkson Show during NBC revealed more information on her talk show about her and Brandon's wedding, revealing that they "totally ended up eloping."

"We had this big plan and then we just took our kids and then got married with this person we didn't even know—but it was the most amazing thing—under a little barn on a river. It was so beautiful!" she shared. "It's cool to make it intimate, because your life is always in the public eye."

The 37-year-old bride donned a white lace long gown with long bohemian style sleeves with tiny buttons and a v-neckline during an intimate ceremony on Sunday. Whilst the groom was wearing black suits pants.

Before Kelly, Brandon was once married to his first wife, Melissa Ashworth, whom he divorced in 2012. 

Brandon and Kelly's First Met: 2006

In 2006, when the singer rehearsed before the Brandon American Country Music Awards, Kelly's manager Narvel Blackstock introduced Kelly to his son Brandon for the first time.

In 2012, she said about their first met with Daily Mail, 

I've known him [Brandon] for six years, but he was married for most of that time,"then, suddenly, there he was at the Super Bowl and he was single…" 

Apparently, future father-in-law Narvel manages Clarkson, and Brandon reintroduced where Kelly performed the National Anthem (NBD) following his son's divorce. They finally kicked off their relationship in 2012 at Super Bowl XLVI.

Engagement Proposal

Aged 43, The Fort Worth, Texas-born manager, proposed his future wife with a yellow canary diamond ring from Johnathon Arndt. The couple got engaged in December 2012 after dating for 10 months.

As the first-ever American Idol winner and current judge on The Voice also tweeted her fans on December 15, 2012. “I wanted y'all to know!! Happiest night of my life last night! I am so lucky and am with the greatest man ever :)".

Father of Four Lovely Kids

The handsome man who stands 5 Feet 8 Inches tall and his present wife, Kelly shares two children from their conjugal life. River Rose Blackstock is the firstborn baby daughter who was born on June 12, 2014.

Two years later, the American singer shared the birth of the duo's second child on media. The baby number two named is Remington Alexander Blackstock who was welcomed on 2016.

In addition, Brandon had already been taking on the role of a great father to his two babies from his previous marriage. He and his ex-wife Melissa are the parents of Savannah Blackstock (aged 17) and Seth Blackstock (aged 11). 

Not only father but also stepmother Kelly share a good bond with them. She turned a red carpet appearance into an adorable family outing with Brandon and all four of their children in April 2019.
