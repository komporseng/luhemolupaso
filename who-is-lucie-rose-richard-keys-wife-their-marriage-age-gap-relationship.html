Who is Lucie Rose (Richard Keys Wife)? Their Marriage, Age Gap, Relationship

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, June 24, 2024

Lucie Rose is a highly accomplished lawyer who currently works as a special funds counsel in the Corporate Department of Proskauer, a prominent London-based firm.

She is recognized as an admitted solicitor in England and Wales and has garnered valuable professional experience, including her early career as an in-house lawyer in the Middle East.

How Did They Met?

Lucie Rose and Richard Keys crossed paths while Keys was residing in Doha, Qatar, working as a presenter for BeIN Sports, a television network. Their introduction likely occurred through a mutual acquaintance, although it is worth noting that Lucie Rose had established a friendship with Keys’ daughter, Jemma.

Following the revelation of Keys’ extramarital affair in 2016, he and his ex-wife ended their marriage. Julia, his former wife, candidly expressed the devastation caused by the affair, as she was recovering from thyroid cancer at the time. The affair had a profound impact on their two children as well.

Keys expressed his frustration with the situation in an interview with The Athletic in 2019, emphasizing that he did not fall in love with a friend of his daughter’s.

Approximately two years ago, Richard Keys reportedly proposed to Lucie Rose, marking a significant milestone in their relationship. Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, their wedding plans were postponed multiple times.

Nevertheless, they recently celebrated their union in a picturesque open-air civil ceremony in Kingswear near Brixham, Devon. The ceremony took place under clear cobalt blue skies and abundant sunshine, providing a memorable backdrop for their special day.

It is evident that Lucie Rose has established herself as a talented lawyer, while Richard Keys continues to make a name for himself in the world of sports presenting. Together, they have embarked on a new chapter in their lives, demonstrating resilience in the face of challenges and the power of love and commitment.

Richard Keys, a 66-year-old sports pundit, has recently married Lucie Rose, a 35-year-old woman who happened to be his daughter’s former best friend. The marriage between Richard Keys and Lucie Rose has drawn attention due to the circumstances surrounding their relationship. Richard Keys’s ex-wife, Julia Keys, filed for divorce in 2016, citing adultery and alleging that her husband had engaged in an affair with Lucie Rose.

The emotional turmoil caused by the affair deeply affected Julia Keys.Notably, Richard Keys sent a text message to his ex-wife just two hours before his marriage to Lucie Rose, informing her of his new marital status.

The marriage between Richard Keys and Lucie Rose has attracted significant media attention and sparked discussions regarding the complexities of their relationship and the aftermath of Richard Keys’s divorce from Julia Keys.
