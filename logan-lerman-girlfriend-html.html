Meet Logan Lermans Girlfriend Analuisa Corrigan

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, May 29, 2024

After a fifteen-year hiatus not from movies but television series, Logan Lerman returned with Hunters, playing protagonist Jonah Heidelbaum.

Lerman’s character lives in New York City in 1979 and knows little about Auschwitz during World War II, the tragedy that frames the series. 

But all that changes when his grandmother is murdered. In order to get revenge, he joins an eclectic underground band of Nazi hunters led by Holocaust survivor Meyer Offerman (Al Pacino).

According to showrunner David Weil, Lerman was more than just an actor in the series. He was “the beating heart of the production” who worked hard to help create a collaborative set by producing and giving ideas for scenes. 

The spotlight had been off The Perks of Being a Wallflower star for a while before Hunters, but with season 2 well underway, the actor is immersed in his work. And when he is not working, he is busy with his girlfriend. For those out of the loop on “Who is Logan Lerman dating,” here is a breakdown of the actor’s love life. 

Logan Lerman’s Girlfriend

Lerman is currently in a relationship with Analuisa Corrigan. However, it is unconfirmed when the two started dating, as they are private about their relationship and hardly post about each other on social media. 

However, we know they have been together since the beginning of last year as Corrigan uploaded a series of adorable snaps of her and Lerman in honor of the actor’s birthday. She kept the caption short and sweet with “Happy Logie Day.”

Corrigan is an artist based in Los Angeles and New York. She graduated with a BFA in Communication Designs from Parsons, The New School of Design. She has been working within several mediums for the past ten years, including clay, paint, and printed matter.

Much of her ceramic artwork is featured on her Instagram. But she had no intention of pursuing ceramic art when she became an artist. Instead, after being bored with her sedentary lifestyle as an intern at an advertising agency, she bought a bag of clay on a whim and started playing with it. 

She found it therapeutic and soon realized she wanted to grow her craft. According to her website, as a ceramic artist, her work is about the “fragility and integrity of clay as a material and practice.” Her abstract-shaped lamps are commercially available. 

Logan Lerman’s Engagement

Before Corrigan, Lerman almost made a wife out of his Percy Jackson co-star Alexandra Daddario. 

The couple was private about their relationship; therefore, the timeline of their relationship is a little hazy. However, they were spotted a few times in different cities in the U.S, and according to Inquisitr, they were even engaged in 2016. 

However, the Baywatch star and the Fury actor broke off their engagement and moved on with their lives. Daddario shared with Women’s Health in May 2017 that she was single and actively dating, and currently, she is dating producer Andrew Form. 

The actress is not coy about divulging the details of her relationship as she often shares photos of her with Form on Instagram.
